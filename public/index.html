How to protect yourself
Never share your OTP
No bank, government body, or company will ever call and ask for your OTP or password.
Urgency is a warning sign
"Act in 24 hours or your account will be blocked" is pressure to stop you thinking. Slow down.
Check links carefully
Scammers use lookalike domains. hdfc-kyc.net is NOT hdfcbank.com.
No free money
KBC prizes, lottery wins, and "earn ₹50,000/day" offers are almost always scams.
Hang up and call back
Got a suspicious call? Hang up. Find the official number yourself and call back to verify.
